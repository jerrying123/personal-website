I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Mechanical Engineering in the d'Arbeloff Laboratory at MIT supervised by Haruhiko Harry Asada. My research explores the use of robots to automate workforce development in manufacturing. I have a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology and an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from MIT. I have worked as a research engineer for NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory and Sandia National Laboratories.
